One Platform · Four Roles

Built for everyone in the loop

Each role gets a purpose-built interface — while every ticket flows through a single, accountable system.

Mobile App
📱

Citizen Application

Report and track local civic issues in a few taps, secured by mobile + OTP login.

  • Create tickets with photo & GPS location
  • Track status with a full timeline history
  • Request escalation after 15 days unresolved
  • Rate service & reopen if the problem returns
Web Portal
🏛

Government Staff Portal

A clean administrative console to triage, assign, and resolve tickets efficiently.

  • Powerful ticket table with search & filters
  • Assign, reassign, escalate & set priority
  • Internal staff-only notes (hidden from citizens)
  • Verify completion before closing tickets
Field Mobile
🔧

Field Technician Interface

Technicians see only their assigned work, with navigation and progress uploads.

  • View assigned tickets with priority & location
  • Open maps for on-site navigation
  • Upload progress & completion photos
  • Submit for government verification — no direct close
Dashboard
📈

MLA Dashboard

Constituency-level oversight and analytics — not day-to-day ticket handling.

  • Overview metrics & visual analytics
  • Department performance & resolution times
  • Ward/area heatmaps & escalated issues
  • Downloadable monthly & area reports
